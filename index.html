★★★★★ (Food: 4/5 | Service: 4/5 | Atmosphere: 5/5)

Visited The Treehouse Café with three friends, and the experience delivered real value across the board. The drinks were the star performers—every single one hit the brief. We ordered the Orange Blossom, a lychee-based drink, a blueberry drink, and a peach iced tea, and all four were genuinely outstanding.

We also tried their cheesy chicken pops, and those were a major win... The pizza was on the smaller side, but they compensated with a generous overload of chicken and sausage. The ambience is full open-air, which creates a nice vibe.

Overall, The Treehouse Café delivers strong ROI on flavour, atmosphere, and service. Great drinks, great snacks, and a vibe that makes you want to return. Definitely heading back to try more.
